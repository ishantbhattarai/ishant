Ingredients: For the Pizza Dough: 1 and 1/2 cups warm water (around 110째F or 45째C) 2 teaspoons active dry yeast 1 teaspoon sugar 4 cups all-purpose flour 1 teaspoon salt 2 tablespoons olive oil For the Pizza Toppings: 1 cup pizza sauce 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese Your favorite pizza toppings (e.g., pepperoni, bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives, etc.) Fresh basil leaves (optional, for garnish) Grated Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish) Instructions: 1. Prepare the Pizza Dough: In a small bowl, combine warm water, active dry yeast, and sugar. Stir gently and let it sit for about 5-10 minutes until it becomes frothy and activates. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Make a well in the center of the flour mixture and pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Stir everything together until a dough forms. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes until it becomes smooth and elastic. You can also use a stand mixer with a dough hook for this step. Place the dough in a greased bowl, cover it with a damp cloth or plastic wrap, and let it rise in a warm place for about 1-1.5 hours or until it has doubled in size. 2. Preheat the Oven: Preheat your oven to 475째F (245째C). If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while it preheats. 3. Shape the Pizza: After the dough has risen, punch it down to release the air. Divide it into two equal portions for two 12-inch pizzas or leave it whole for one large pizza. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness. 4. Add the Toppings: Place the rolled-out dough on a pizza peel or a baking sheet if you don't have a pizza stone. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce. Add your favorite toppings. Be creative! 5. Bake: If using a pizza stone, carefully transfer the pizza onto the hot stone in the preheated oven. Bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned. 6. Garnish and Serve: Remove the pizza from the oven and let it cool for a minute or two. If desired, garnish with fresh basil leaves and grated Parmesan cheese. Slice the pizza and serve hot. Enjoy your homemade pizza! Feel free to customize your homemade pizza with your favorite ingredients and experiment with different sauces, cheeses, and toppings to create the perfect pizza for your taste.
