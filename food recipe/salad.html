Ingredients: For the Salad: 4 ripe tomatoes, cut into wedges or chunks 1 cucumber, sliced 1 red onion, thinly sliced 1 green bell pepper, sliced 1 red bell pepper, sliced 1 cup Kalamata olives, pitted 1 cup crumbled feta cheese Fresh oregano leaves or dried oregano (for garnish) For the Dressing: 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar 1 teaspoon dried oregano (or 1 tablespoon fresh oregano, chopped) 1 clove garlic, minced Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste Instructions: 1. Prepare the Dressing: In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, red wine vinegar, minced garlic, dried oregano (or fresh oregano), salt, and black pepper. Set the dressing aside to allow the flavors to meld. 2. Assemble the Salad: In a large salad bowl, combine the tomato wedges, cucumber slices, sliced red onion, green bell pepper, and red bell pepper. Add the Kalamata olives to the salad. Gently toss the salad ingredients together to mix them evenly. 3. Add the Feta Cheese: Crumble the feta cheese over the top of the salad. 4. Dress the Salad: Drizzle the prepared dressing over the salad. 5. Garnish: Sprinkle fresh oregano leaves or additional dried oregano on top for garnish. 6. Serve: Toss the salad gently one more time just before serving to ensure that the dressing coats all the ingredients. Serve your Greek Salad immediately as a refreshing and healthy appetizer, side dish, or even a light main course. Enjoy your homemade Greek Salad with its combination of fresh vegetables, briny olives, creamy feta cheese, and zesty dressing! It's perfect for a light and flavorful meal, especially on a hot day.
